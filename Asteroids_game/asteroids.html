Instruction to play the game
1. Press "I" to move forward, "K" to move backward, "J" to rotate left and "L" to rotate right. Press Spacebar to shoot.
2. In every 5 seconds, one asteroid will be spawned from the top left corner of the canva.
3. Big asteroids when destroyed will spawn 2 small asteroids. You need to shoot the small asteroids to destroy the asteroid entirely.
4. For every big asteroid you destroy, you get 10 points. For every small asteroid you destroy, you get 5 points.
5. If your spaceship collides with either big or small asteroid, your life count will decrease by 1.
6. You have 3 lives initially, when your life count turns 0. You lose the game.
7. There is a special ability of your bullet. Your bullet will not disappear after you shoot. It will continue to destroy other asteroids in its trajectory.
8. Press the 'Start' button to start the game. Clicking multiple times wont restart the game.
9. When you hit 30 points, you will enter level 2, 50 points to level 3 and 100 points to level 4. The speed of asteroid spawning will increase one fold every level you up.
10. The only way to win the game is to score as much as possible. However, there is a hidden feasture that the more you score, the slower your bullet is.
11. Lastly, HAVE FUN ! Remember to scroll down to play the game!
